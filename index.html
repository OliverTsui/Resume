Qian Oliver Xu

ROOM302 Hui Oi Chau Science Building· The University of Hong Kong · Hong Kong · oliver_xu@hku.hk

I am a PhD student studying at the Department of Earth Sciences, the University of Hong Kong.
My current research aims to reconstruct the particle fluxes over the last four glacial periods (LGM/MIS2, MIS4, MIS6 and MIS8) and its implication for the presence of sea ice cover, paleocrystic and/or ice shelf in the western Arctic Ocean. Two particle reactive radionuclides 230Th and 231Pa will be used.
